# Sprint 2 comparison — TUI frame parity

Status: **GREEN for deterministic Sprint 2 parity**. Every TUI component, application frame,
interactive command, and `ctx.ui` lifecycle surface renders byte-identical to executable TypeScript
pi on the pinned commit, within the 16 ms frame budget by a factor of thirty. Real-terminal image
emission (kitty/iTerm2) and native-desktop clipboard smoke remain owner-blocked evidence; their
encodings and command profiles are byte-tested. This report is the frame-diff deliverable required
by SPRINTS.md and supersedes `wp-410-report.md` and `tui-gate-report.md` as the current statement.

## Revisions and method

- pi-go base: `b1a61ec` (Sprint 1 closure); candidate: the Sprint 2 closure containing this report.
- upstream: pi `0.80.10`, `3da591ab74ab9ab407e72ed882600b2c851fae21` (UPSTREAM.lock).
- Every frame golden is generated by TypeScript extraction scripts driving the real upstream
  renderer/interactive internals in `.upstream/`, then replayed through the Go port and compared
  byte-for-byte (raw ANSI line count + SHA-256, plus normalized readable lines). `make
  fixtures-check` regenerates the full tree byte-clean.
- Extraction is environment-independent as of this sprint: `generate.ts` strips the invoking
  terminal's `COLORTERM`, the visible-command runner pins 256-color mode, and the session-selector
  fixture uses a deterministic cwd. Regeneration no longer depends on the invoking machine.
- No live provider or real-terminal claim is made anywhere in this report.

## Deviations found and fixed

Landing the lifecycle family surfaced two real concurrency deviations from upstream's
single-threaded semantics, both fixed rather than ledgered: extension-editor swaps are now guarded
by the mode mutex against dialog teardown, and working-indicator lookup+mutation is atomic against
dispose so `Loader.SetIndicator`'s ticker restart cannot resurrect a detached indicator. Resize and
bracketed-paste storms are fuzzed under `-race` (`FuzzTUIResizePaste`, `FuzzStdinBufferChunkedPaste`,
`FuzzTUIRendererLineAndOutput`, `FuzzEditorRenderLineWidths`), clean on the seeded corpora.

**Divergence-ledger delta for Sprint 2: zero new entries.** The only TUI-adjacent ledger lines
remain the pre-existing `/share` local-HTML-export neutralization (declared in the
F12-visible-commands manifest) and the darwin modifier-key native-addon gap.
