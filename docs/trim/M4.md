## Size and cold-start — release risks

Build shapes as before; the product now links sobek + esbuild (the bridge is wired into `cmd/pi`).

| Metric | M3 | M4 candidate (bridged) | Change |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Default binary | 47,238,108 B | 70,315,578 B | +48.8% |
| Stripped binary | 35,102,882 B | 51,425,442 B | +46.5% |
| No-prompt cold-start mean (default) | 44.6 ± 5.8 ms | 50.9 ± 6.2 ms | +14.1% |
| No-prompt cold-start mean (stripped) | — | 50.4 ± 6.9 ms | — |

Investigated, not dismissed: `GODEBUG=inittrace=1` attributes ~9 ms of init to pre-existing
packages (chroma lexers 6.2 ms — unchanged since M2); the bridge packages contribute no init cost
and the no-prompt path constructs no VM. The regression is load-side: a 46–49% larger text/pclntab
image costs ~6 ms of additional page-in/relocation on this hardware. Consequences:

- **At this checkpoint the 50 ms cold-start cap was breached by ~1 ms at the mean** (the 39.9 ms
  minimum showed that the warm floor was fine); **the then-current decimal 35 MB cap was exceeded
  by 16.4 MB.** Neither criterion was weakened here: both stood as written, and M5 could not close
  until the owner decided
  (`docs/plan/expansion-study.md` decision 3): raise the caps for the bridged binary, or ship two
  artifacts (`pi` bridged / `pi-slim` bridge-less build-tag variant, which measured 35.1 MB and
  ~44 ms at M3), or fund deeper size work.
- Recorded in PROGRESS.md as an owner decision with the measured numbers.

Resolution recorded after this historical measurement: on 2026-07-20 the owner retained D17,
adopted a 55 MB decimal bridged-binary cap, and accepted the later controlled cold-start result of
33.5 ± 4.8 ms against the unchanged 50 ms cap.
